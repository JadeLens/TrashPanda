Residential Buildings Pack 2.2
==============================

Thank you for purchasing the Residential Buildings bundle!

Everything in the prefabs folder is fitted with colliders, proper pivot positions, street lights with spotlights nested - all ready to use.

Resolution is configured for standalone build - for mobile, decrease atlases texture resolution to 2048x2048, other textures can be halved too. Don't reduce 'Road_Side' (NPOT) texture size.
